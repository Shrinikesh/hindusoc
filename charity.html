About the Charity

 

Bhagini Nivedita Pune, established over 35 years ago, works for the upliftment of women and children in India, with a special emphasis on socially and economically weaker sections of the society. The organisation enables them to acquire skills for a better life.

Through various activities such as field trips, workshops, and science experiments, they help children strengthen their educational base and motivate them to learn. Their mobile science labs promote childrenâ€™s interest in the world of science.

In addition, the charity runs vocational courses to train women to make them self sufficient in modern society. Some of these include Design, Embroidery, Fashion designing and PCB assembly and packaging. Their computer courses provide the skills required for jobs requiring IT literacy.

Bhagini Nivedita also provides regular health checkups in rural areas and guidance for early prevention of ailments. There are also clinics for speech therapy for deaf and speech-impaired children.

Thank you very much for helping this wonderful charity to provides welfare for women and children, your support means a great deal to the charity and to the many they support.
