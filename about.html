About Mastana

The Show

Every year, the show showcases a wide range of talent, not only from students but also from members of the local community. In the past we've had an array of dances, from classical Indian to break-dancers. We've had sensational singers and musicians, an exquisite fashion show and hilarious comedians. All rounded off by superb acting in a dramatic, captivating and comical story throughout the show.

This year is no exception and we promise to bring you the best that Cambridge has to offer. Don't forget that there will also be food and drink given out during the interval. A full line up of the show will be posted here soon. Keep watching this space.

The Venue

Directions

The Guildhall is on Market Hill, overlooking the Market Square. The ticket entrance is located at the side entrance on Peas Hill.

By Train

The Guildhall is approx. a 20 minute walk from the train station. Upon leaving the station go straight down Station Road and turn right onto Hills Road. Walk down Hills Road which leads on to Regent Street. Turn left into Sidney Street and then immediately left down Petty Curry which leads onto Market Hill.

Parking

There are a number of car parks in Cambridge city centre

  • Grand Arcade Car Park is just a couple of minutes away from Guildhall.
  • Park Street Car Park is no more than a 5 - 10 minutes - walk out of the car park straight down Trinity Street into Kings Parade, left into St. Mary's Street and then right towards Market Hill / Peas Hill.
  • Slightly further away but again no more than a 10 minute walk is Queen Anne Car Park in Gonville Place; head out of the car park, down Regent's Terrace (runs alongside the big green space called Parker's Piece) into Regent Street, left into Petty Curry which leads onto Market Hill.
  • Additional city car parks are Grafton East in East Road and Grafton West in Newmarket Road.
